Frequently Asked Questions


What kind of events qualify for reimbursement?

The events that are covered by the reimbursement plan are: university courses, seminars, certification preparation classes, certifications, technical training and other. For more information on other, contact the benefit coordinator.

How much can I getreimbursement?

The total amount an employee can claim is $1,000.00 in tuition reimbursment per year. The percentage per type of event is as follows:

  • University Courses - 80%
  • Seminars - 60%
  • Certification Preparation Classes - 75%
  • Certification - 100%
  • Technical Training - 90%
  • Other - 30%

What if I am denied reimbursement?

Contact the indivdual who denied your application and look into why it was denied. Try again.

What do I do after classes are over?

You must submit either the final grade or a presentation for your application to be review for reimbursement.
