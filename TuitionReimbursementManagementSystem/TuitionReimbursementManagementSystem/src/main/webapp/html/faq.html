Frequently Asked Questions


What kind of events qualify for reimbursement?

The events that are covered by the reimbursement plan are: university courses, seminars, certification preparation classes, certifications, technical training and other. For more information on other, contact the benefit coordinator.

How much can I get reimbursement?

The total amount an employee can claim is $1,000.00 in tuition reimbursement per year. The percentage of total cost that will be reimbursed is as follows:

  • University Courses - 80%
  • Seminars - 60%
  • Certification Preparation Classes - 75%
  • Certification - 100%
  • Technical Training - 90%
  • Other - 30%

What if I am denied reimbursement?

Contact the individual who denied your application and look into why it was denied. Try again.

What do I do after the event is over?

You must submit either your final grade or a presentation for your application to be review for reimbursement. This is dependent on the event, itself.

What if I have pre-approval for reimbursement for an event?

Make sure you have an email from your direct supervisor or department head explicitly giving pre-approval for your event. Save and attach this email to the reimbursement application form.

How do I check the status of my reimbursement application?

All reimbursement applications should be visible on your TRMS homepage after you log in.

My event is over. Why is my application still pending?

Make sure you submitted your proof of completion, which is either your final grade or a presentation.
